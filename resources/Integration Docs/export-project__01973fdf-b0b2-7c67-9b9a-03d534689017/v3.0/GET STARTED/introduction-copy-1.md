### Checkout Comparison

<details>

<summary>Click Here to View</summary>

<br />

The table below helps you to compare the features of Plural Hosted and Seamless Checkout Solutions.

| Feature                 | Plural Hosted Checkout                                                                                                         | Plural iFrame Checkout                                                                                           | Seamless Checkout                                                                      |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Payment Flow**        | Customers are taken to Plural's secure hosted payment page in a new browser tab, ensuring a smooth and secure payment journey. | Customers complete their payments securely on Plural’s payment page, which opens within a frame on your website. | Customers stay on your website for the entire payment process.                         |
| **Customization**       | Plural handles the UI and security. options to customize the logo and theme.                                                   | Plural handles the UI and security. options to customize the logo and theme.                                     | Full control over the checkout UI and experience.                                      |
| **PCI Compliance**      | Handled by Plural. You don’t need to have a PCI compliance certificate.                                                        | Handled by Plural. You don’t need to have a PCI compliance certificate.                                          | You must obtain PCI compliance certification to process card payments.                 |
| **Ease of Integration** | Quick and easy to integrate with minimal technical effort.                                                                     | Quick and easy to integrate with minimal technical effort.                                                       | Flexibility to build your own checkout and integrate seamlessly with our payment APIs. |
| **Payment Methods**     | All supported methods by Plural are available on the hosted page.                                                              | All supported methods by Plural are available on the iFame.                                                      | You select which payment methods to offer and integrate them.                          |
| **User Experience**     | Plural manages the entire checkout experience.                                                                                 | Plural manages the entire checkout experience.                                                                   | You have complete control and customization options over the checkout experience.      |
